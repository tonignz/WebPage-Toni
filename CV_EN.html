Software Quality Assurance (SQA)

February 2021 - March 2023

Throughout my as an SQA, I gained extensive knowledge of communication protocols such as CAN and LIN. Specifically, I became proficient in the J1939 protocol, a specialized automotive standard that defines the upper layers of the OSI model. Additionally, I worked extensively with the CANopen protocol, particularly under the CiA 301 and CiA 402 standards. In the realm of LIN, I focused on proprietary LIN implementations and standards defined by clients. My responsibilities included defining functional and communication tests for both complete systems and specific components.

I utilized tools such as BUSMASTER, KVASER, and EMOTAS for testing, as well as error management and collaboration tools like Jira and Subversion repositories. Furthermore, I continued to deepen my knowledge of programming languages such as Python and LabVIEW. In addition to my core responsibilities, I conducted extensive data analysis to decode communication bus behaviors and identify errors, anomalies, or trends in the tested devices. These data analysis techniques not only facilitated error detection but also served as a basis for laboratory improvements, enabling more precise and comprehensive data collection from various test benches.

I also actively contributed to the development of numerous applications in LabVIEW. These applications were not limited to testing purposes but were also leveraged across departments, subsidiaries, and even by end clients. By creating user-friendly and efficient LabVIEW applications, I facilitated smoother operations and enhanced productivity throughout the organization.

Moreover, I had the opportunity to work directly with clients, honing my skills in customer orientation and negotiation. This involved collaborating with international clients, sometimes traveling to their facilities to provide on-site support and address their needs. These interactions not only enhanced my technical expertise but also allowed me to develop strong interpersonal skills and a deep understanding of client requirements in the automotive industry.
